## Finger Placement

The **G major** chord uses three fingers and rings all six strings open or fretted.

```diagram
type: chord
title: G Major
frets: [3, 2, 0, 0, 0, 3]
fingers: [2, 1, null, null, null, 3]
```

Place your **index finger** on the 2nd fret of the A string (string 5).
Place your **middle finger** on the 3rd fret of the low E string (string 6).
Place your **ring finger** on the 3rd fret of the high e string (string 1).

## Common Mistakes

- Muting the B string with the ring finger — keep the finger tip arched
- Not pressing close enough to the fret wire — causes fret buzz

## Practice Tips

1. Place fingers one at a time before strumming
2. Pluck each string individually to check it rings clearly
