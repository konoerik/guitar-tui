## The Exercise

Play frets 1–2–3–4 on every string from low E to high e, then back down.
One finger per fret: index (1), middle (2), ring (3), pinky (4).

After completing all 6 strings going up, reverse: high e back down to low E.

## Rules

- Use a metronome. Start at **60 BPM**, eighth notes.
- Every note must ring cleanly — no buzzing. Do not move faster until it is clean.
- Keep unused fingers hovering close to the fretboard (not flying away).
- Once comfortable at a position, shift the pattern up one fret (2–3–4–5).

## Common Mistakes

**Lifting the hand off the neck between strings.** Keep the wrist steady and
move only the fingers.

**Skipping the pinky.** The pinky is the weakest finger and needs the most
work. Do not cheat by substituting the ring finger on fret 4.
